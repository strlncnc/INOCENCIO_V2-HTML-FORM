Digital Accessibility Statement


Gourmet Grounds believe in fostering connections with every cup and every interaction. That's why we're dedicated to ensuring that all our digital platforms are accessible to everyone, including our valued customers. We follow the guidelines set forth by the World wide Web Consortium (W3C), including Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) Levels A and AA, as well as compliance with relevant laws susch as the Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA), U.S. Section 508 of the Rehabilitation Act of 1973, and other accessibility regulations.

Our efforts are ongoing, and we continuously work with internal and external partners to enhance the accessibility of our digital properties

if you have any questions, feedback, or experience difficulties accessing our website

Please contact us through our email.

Please note that while we strive to make our platforms as accessible as possible, some challenges may arise due to the diverse range of assistive technologies available.

We appreciate your understandingand remain committed to improving accessibility for all.
